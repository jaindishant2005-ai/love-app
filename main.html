This isn’t something you need to understand.
It’s something you’re meant to feel,

For the days you smile,
and for the days you don’t.

Why this exists

I didn’t make this to impress you.
I made it because choosing you
is something I do — consciously,
intentionally,
every single time.

This place exists so you never forget
that you were never an option.

You were chosen.
